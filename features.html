Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using royalty-free music from Davita Bros. Sounds in your projects — from YouTube videos to TikTok, films, and beyond.

What does "royalty-free" actually mean?

Royalty-free means you pay a one-time licensing fee to use a track — no recurring royalty payments, no copyright strikes, no legal headaches. Once you purchase a license from Davita Bros. Sounds, you can use that track in your projects without paying additional fees each time it's played or viewed.

Where can I use music from Davita Bros. Sounds?

Our tracks are licensed for use across a wide range of platforms and formats — including YouTube videos, TikTok content, Instagram Reels, podcasts, short films, documentaries, advertisements, corporate presentations, and more. Specific usage rights are outlined clearly on each license tier.

Who is Davita Bros. Sounds designed for?

We serve a broad community of digital creators — YouTube and TikTok content creators, video editors, indie filmmakers, podcasters, social media managers, game developers, and marketing teams. Whether you're a solo hobbyist or a professional production studio, our catalog has tracks built for your workflow.

Can I preview tracks before purchasing?

Absolutely. Every track in our catalog includes a full-length preview so you can hear exactly how it sounds before committing to a purchase. We want you to feel confident about every track you license — no surprises after checkout.

What license types are available?

We offer tiered licensing to match your project scope — from standard licenses for personal and small-channel use to extended licenses for commercial campaigns, broadcast, and large-scale distribution. Each tier is clearly priced so you only pay for what you actually need.

Will I get a copyright strike using your music on YouTube?

No. Our royalty-free music for YouTube videos is specifically cleared for monetized content. When you purchase a license, you receive documentation confirming your right to use the track. If a Content ID claim is ever filed in error, your license serves as proof of authorized use for dispute resolution.

How do I find the right track for my project?

Our catalog is organized by genre, mood, tempo, and instrumentation so you can filter quickly by what your project needs. Looking for something cinematic for a film cut, or high-energy for a TikTok? Use our mood tags and keyword search to narrow down options in seconds — no guesswork required.

What if I need help choosing a track or have a custom request?

Our team is happy to assist. If you're unsure which track fits your project, or if you're looking for something specific that isn't in the catalog, reach out directly — we love working with creators to find the perfect sound for their vision.

Still have questions? We're here to help you find the right sound.
